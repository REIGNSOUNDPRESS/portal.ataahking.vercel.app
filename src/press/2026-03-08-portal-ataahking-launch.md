**GLOBAL ―**  

Reignsound Press is pleased to announce the launch of the **ATAAH KING Public Portal** (`portal.ataahking.vercel.app`), an official centralized hub for global fans, media, and collaborators. This portal is designed to provide **real-time information**, including public schedules, press releases, and upcoming announcements directly managed by Reignsound Press on behalf of ATAAH KING.

**Purpose and Usage**  

The portal serves multiple functions:  

- **Daily Public Schedule**: Displays official ATAAH KING events for today and upcoming events. Events are pulled directly from the **official Reignsound Google Calendar**, ensuring accuracy and reliability.  
- **Press Releases**: Aggregates and displays official press statements, announcements, and updates from Reignsound Press. Each release is accessible in full detail, with date-stamped entries and proper citations.  
- **Global Accessibility**: Designed to serve a worldwide audience with fast-loading pages, mobile-friendly interfaces, and a dark-mode theme for visual comfort.

**Event Management and Maintenance**  

- Events are added to the official Google Calendar (`portal.ataahking.vercel.app` schedule) by authorized personnel at Reignsound Press.  
- Each day, the portal automatically pulls **today’s events plus the next two upcoming events**, highlighting priority or all-day events as needed.  
- Press releases are managed through **Markdown files stored in the portal’s repository** (`/press` folder) and can be updated via GitHub. This ensures that updates are version-controlled, auditable, and instantly reflected on the website.  
- The portal undergoes continuous monitoring and testing to ensure **data accuracy, reliability, and uptime**.

**Development Timeline**  

- Development for this portal began on **March 8, 2026, at 10:00 AM ET**.  
- Core functionality, including schedule display and press release integration, was implemented during the **development window from 10:00 AM to 1:30 PM ET**.  
- Testing and deployment continued from **1:30 PM to 2:00 PM ET**, finalizing the live portal.

**Future Usage and Distribution**  

- The portal will serve as the **primary source of truth** for ATAAH KING public information.  
- Other platforms (e.g., Medium, social media) may post announcements referencing these press releases, but **all official content originates here** to maintain accuracy and brand consistency.
